Privacy Shield Initiative

A student-led awareness campaign addressing the risks of smart glasses, hidden recording, and AI-driven surveillance in public and private spaces.

Issue Risks Impact Solutions Join

The Problem

Smart glasses equipped with cameras can record individuals without consent, raising serious privacy and ethical concerns.

Key Risks

Privacy Violations

People can be recorded without knowing or consenting.

AI Misuse

Data may be used for facial recognition or AI training.

Academic Dishonesty

Smart glasses can be used to cheat in exams.

Surveillance Risks

Potential for stalking, doxxing, and identity tracking.

Awareness Impact

Public Awareness of Smart Glass Risks

Regulation Coverage

Reported Misuse Cases

Proposed Solutions

  • Mandatory recording indicators (LED alerts)
  • Geofencing in sensitive areas
  • Auto face blurring in recordings
  • Clear data usage disclosure
  • Stronger government regulation
